Unique Physical Signatures

Our approach does not
assign identities —
it discovers them

Physical component

Clone-proof, design-flexible labels that serve as physical fingerprints — each product receives a truly unique identity through a stochastic printing process that is scalable, cost-efficient, and production-ready

Digital component

A smartphone-based recognition system powered by a proprietary algorithm inspired by biometric identification — stable across scan conditions, adaptable to any label design, and secure end-to-end
